Angel of Death

by Ryan Green

How many patients must die under a nurse's care to raise suspicions? In 1895, Jane Toppan's patients began dying mysteriously—until an entire family perished. The truth behind her care was far darker than anyone could imagine... Angel of Death tells the chilling story of a killer in disguise.

About the Author

Ryan Green is a true crime author who lives in Herefordshire, England with his wife, three children, and two dogs. Outside of writing and spending time with his family, Ryan enjoys walking, reading, and history. Ryan is fascinated with History, Psychology, and True Crime. In 2015, he finally started researching and writing his own work and at the end of the year, he released his first book on Britain’s most notorious serial killer, Harold Shipman. He has since written several books on lesser-known subjects, including the approach of writing from the killer’s perspective. He narrates some of the chilling scenes you’ll encounter in the True Crime genre. "Ryan Green is an incredible storyteller... He will leave you, and you will love to be part of it." --Blackbird

About the Author

Julie L. Brown is the author of the alternative-history novel, No One Will Save Us and the creator, under the pen name J. L.
